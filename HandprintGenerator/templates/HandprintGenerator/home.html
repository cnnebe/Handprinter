Handprinting is about trying to ensure that the world is better off with us than without us.

Did you know? If just 25% of U.S. families used 10 fewer plastic bags a month, we would save over 2.5 BILLION bags a year!

The Handprinter website and a growing family of apps are designed to empower all people to inform and encourage one another to make these changes, to be the groundswell we wish to see in the world.
